My Two Best Friends: Riley & Goose

Riley was adopted from Charming Pet Rescue in Boerne, Texas. For this reason, she has a special jingle written about her: "She's the bestest in the Westest, 'cause this girly is from Texas!"

Goose hails from North Carolina. He loves to fetch, swim, and steal licks of whatever his parents are eating.
